About Test Product

Learn about our journey, mission, and commitment to excellence in business intelligence.

Our Story

Founded in 2018 by a team of data scientists and business strategists, Test Product was born from a simple observation: businesses were drowning in data but struggling to extract actionable insights. We recognized that companies needed a solution that was both powerful and accessible. Starting in a small office with just five team members, we built Test Product to democratize advanced analytics and make enterprise-grade tools available to businesses of all sizes. Today, we've grown to a team of over 150 professionals serving more than 10,000 active users across 50+ countries, all united by our passion for helping businesses succeed.

Our Mission & Values

Our mission is to empower every organization with the tools, insights, and support they need to make smarter decisions and achieve their goals. We believe in transparency, innovation, and customer-first thinking. We're committed to maintaining the highest standards of security and privacy while continuously evolving our platform to meet the changing needs of modern businesses. Our core values—integrity, excellence, collaboration, and customer success—guide every decision we make. We don't just provide software; we partner with our clients to drive real, measurable business outcomes and foster long-term relationships built on trust and mutual success.
